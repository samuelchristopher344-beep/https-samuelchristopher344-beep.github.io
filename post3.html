Common Mistakes New Website Owners Make

Starting a website can be exciting, but beginners often make mistakes that slow growth or cause technical problems.

Common mistakes include: using unclear navigation, missing essential pages, not optimizing for mobile, or having duplicate content.

Avoid these mistakes by planning your site structure, writing original content, and following best practices for SEO and user experience.
