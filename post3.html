Common Mistakes New Website Owners Make

Many beginners make mistakes when creating their first website. One common mistake is publishing very little content and expecting quick results.

Another mistake is copying content from other websites. Search engines prefer original content written in your own words.

Poor navigation is also a problem. Visitors should be able to move easily between pages without confusion.

Building a successful website takes time, patience, and learning. Focusing on quality and consistency helps avoid these mistakes.
